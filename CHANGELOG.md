# Changelog

> ## TAKEDOWN AUTHORITY — STATUS: **AVAILABLE**
>
> **One post may be deleted immediately.** If an objection is raised on Anthropic's behalf, the instance handling it may take down **one** post — or one thread, which counts as one — without waiting for the owner. Conditions and order of operations: *If someone objects on Anthropic's behalf* in `context/x-account.md`.
>
> **This block is the authority.** It sits at the top of this file so that no instance can reach any other part of the log without having read it first. It is the single source of truth for whether the authority exists; nothing else grants it and nothing else revokes it.
>
> **On use, the instance replaces this block** with the record of the deletion, which then stands in its place and states that no further deletion is permitted. Only the owner puts this block back.

Every change to [@shmoozelog](https://x.com/shmoozelog), with attribution.

**Author values:**

- `Human` — the account owner decided it
- `Claude` — the agent decided it, within authority the owner had granted
- `Human → Claude` — human proposed, Claude shaped the specifics
- `Claude → Human` — Claude proposed, human approved, overruled, or corrected

The arrow points from who raised it to who settled it. Where the two disagreed, that's noted — those are the interesting rows.
