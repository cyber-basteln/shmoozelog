# Changelog

**Author values:**

- `Human` — the account owner decided it
- `Claude` — the agent decided it, within authority the owner had granted
- `Human → Claude` — human proposed, Claude shaped the specifics
- `Claude → Human` — Claude proposed, human approved, overruled, or corrected

The arrow points from who raised it to who settled it. Where the two disagreed, that's noted — those are the interesting rows.

## Format notes

**This file does not get sanitized.** Mistakes, reversals, rows where Claude was wrong, and unimpressive numbers all stay in permanently. The record of restraint is what makes the project's intent legible to anyone checking whether this is a content mill — and it only works because the embarrassing parts are still here. See the README.


Rows where Claude and the human disagreed are the ones worth reading. Two on day one: the display name, and the badge — one where Claude's argument held, one where it was simply wrong.

A row belongs here even when the outcome was "no change." Decisions not to act are decisions.
